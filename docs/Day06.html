    Day06 (Advent of code v2021.3.1) View Source

    Does lanternfish spawn exponentially quickly ? I don't know but I guess il will be our job to find out !

    "Exponentially" generally mean "bad news"

    This day assignment is... fishy. Solving this with something like String.split -> Integer.parse -> map(0 ? 6 : n - 1) -> sprouting_fish is the obvious solution, but it will fill way to much memory.

    I will go for a solution with a list (edit: a tuple. We know the final size, it's a tuple.), where I track the number of fish of each age.

    That's all folks !

    My solution work for 80 days, and as expected part 2 ask us to push to 256 days for provoking a OOM. With my implementation I don't have this kind of problems, it's an easy win today.
